About Me

Howdy! My name’s Jameson - a curious mind learning full stack web development. I have experience in mobile apps, oil & gas IoT sensors, and software sales.

In 2015, I graduated from the University of Texas at Austin with a degree in Economics & Business. While in school, I started a software company called Flexter Inc., developing mobile applications for fitness influencers.

My responsibilities included design, product and project management of the entire development cycle from inception to launch. I contracted developers overseas in Russia and Bulgaria to build the app. I’d then license the use of this code to trainers in a profit share agreement. My company was accepted into Capital Factory’s Accelerator program and has since generated over $300k+ in sales.

In my spare time - I love to find and share great music on SoundCloud, experience the great outdoors, play golf, hang out with my fiends, and learn to code.
